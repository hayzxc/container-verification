1.1 Color Palette (Substrate & Ink)

  • Substrate: #F4F4F0 (Matte Documentation Paper)

1.2 Typography

  • Headings: Inter Black (or Neue Haas Grotesk). Negative tracking, compressed leading.
  • Telemetry/Data: IBM Plex Mono. Uppercase, generous tracking.
