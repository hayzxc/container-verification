Design Spec: Section 2 - Component Architecture

Tactical redesign of interface elements and Dark Mode logic.

Component: Tactical Button
Industrial Inversion (Dark Mode)
MODE: INVERTED_DOC_PRINT

SYSTEM
READY

> SESSION_ID: 0x88F2
> QUEUE_DEPTH: 12

2.1 Component Directives

  • Buttons: Zero radius. Solid backgrounds or heavy 2px borders. Use ASCII markers like [ ] or → for tactical feel.
  • Inputs: Flat background, 1px bottom border only, or full 1px box. Monospace text input.
  • Cards: Replaced by "Blocks." No shadows. Delineated by 1px solid grid lines.

2.2 Dark Mode: Industrial Inversion

  • Transition: Absolute color flip. Substrate becomes #0A0A0A, Ink becomes #EAEAEA.
  • Aviation Red: Remains constant (#E61919) for both modes to maintain hazard consistency.
